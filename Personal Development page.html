My Personal Development

My Strengths Include:
  • Determination
  • Adaptation
  • Patience

The ability to keep going when things look a bit rough or difficult to continue is a feature that I would like to pinpoint as one of my strength. This determination have help me through some really difficult times over the last 5 years of my life. This feature that I cultivated over these years have contributed to personal growth in ways that improve how
I handle certain situation and it made me more aware of my limitations and capabilities. Identifying your limitation is the start to acknowledging your weakness.
However, my ability to quickly adopt to circumstances or environmental changes help towards my growth as I tried to become a more mature adult.
Being an adult, patience is a very vital feature to have. The ability to be humble enough to wait on something or wise enough to know when to take risks help me over these past 5 years.
Patience was never one of my strongest feature, however over time I learnt the virtue of absolute patience and now I can go through life knowing that whatever I put my mind to will pay off in time.
